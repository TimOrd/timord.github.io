Free programs

At Calisthenics Club we want fitness to be accessible to all, so we have put together some 30 day programs that are completely free.

We have 3 programs to choose from

At Calisthenics Club we want fitness to be accessible to all, so we have put together some 30 day programs that are completely free.

There is a choice of 3 to choose from and if you do all of them that is 90 days of workouts!

Bodyweight beginner

A free 30 day program taking you through the basics of bodyweight exercises, able to be done anywhere at anytime. This will build a strong foundation for programs to follow.

It includes leg, chest, bicep, tricep, shoulder and back exercises to give you a full body workout.

There are also dedicated stretch sessions included to help you recover.

Start today

Path to pull ups

A free program to get you started on your journey to pull ups.

This program requires no specialist kit and can be done from the comfort of your home. It builds the strength needed to do your first ever pull up! You will work on your core, shoulders, back muscles and more.

The program is designed to work major muscles and accessory exercises to work the smaller muscles that will support your path to a pull up.

Start today
